Body of Work

The thinking behind the work.

A foundational white paper, concept work on the care economy, a 2025 TEDx talk, articles, ongoing writing, and a forthcoming book — developed over years of practice across industry, entrepreneurship, and education.

Foundational Paper
  • Redefining the American Dream Ownership, Democracy, and Shared Prosperity. The foundational white paper of the Shared Prosperity Model — the argument that prosperity is now determined by asset ownership, not wages, and that democracy requires predistribution rather than redistribution to remain viable.
    White Paper · 2025
Concept Work
  • Building a New Care Economy A Worker-to-Owner enterprise-level exploration. Concept paper applying the Shared Prosperity architecture to California's care economy — testing whether redesigned enterprise structures, shared services, and worker ownership pathways can stabilize a sector facing a structural crisis of affordability, workforce, and healthspan.
    Concept Paper · 2026
Articles & Essays
  • A Nation of Entrepreneurs: Our Most Undervalued Asset On the 33 million entrepreneurs who carry the American Dream — and the structural reasons most never make the leap from self-employment to wealth-building enterprise. The case for a bell-curve economy over winner-takes-all.
    Article · 2024
  • Deschooling the World On Ivan Illich's image of "educational webs" — and what it would mean to build learning infrastructure that distributes agency rather than credential.
    Substack · 2025
Talks
